**Add a player bookings page, booking cancellation, and a proper post-payment confirmation screen.**

---

### 1. My Games Page (`/my-games`)

Create a page where logged-in players can see all their bookings. Redirect to `/auth?returnUrl=/my-games` if not logged in.

**Layout:**
- Page title: "My Games"
- Two tabs/sections: "Upcoming" and "Past"
- Default to showing "Upcoming"

**Each booking card shows:**
- Game title
- Pitch name
- Date and kickoff time
- Team assignment (Team 1 or Team 2)
- Payment status (green "Paid" badge)
- Spots filled (e.g. "7/10")
- A "View Game" button that links to `/game/[id]`
- A "Cancel Booking" button (only for upcoming games, see cancellation rules below)

**Upcoming tab:** Games where kickoff_time is in the future, sorted by soonest first.

**Past tab:** Games where kickoff_time has passed, sorted by most recent first. No cancel button on past games.

**Empty state:** If the player has no bookings, show "No games yet" with a link to "Browse Games" (`/games`).

---

### 2. Cancel Booking Flow

Add a "Cancel Booking" button on each upcoming booking card in the My Games page AND on the game detail page (`/game/[id]`) when the player has an active booking.

**When the player clicks "Cancel Booking":**

Show a confirmation modal/dialog that displays the refund tier based on how much time is left before kickoff:

- **More than 12 hours before kickoff:** "You will receive a 100% refund of SAR [amount]."
- **6 to 12 hours before kickoff:** "You will receive a 50% refund of SAR [amount]. SAR [other half] will not be refunded."
- **Less than 6 hours before kickoff:** "No refund is available. Your booking cannot be cancelled less than 6 hours before kickoff."

If no refund is available (<6 hours), still show the modal but make it a warning. Let the player decide whether to proceed — do NOT block cancellation entirely, just make the no-refund consequence very clear before they confirm.

**Modal buttons:**
- "Keep My Spot" (dismiss)
- "Cancel Booking" (red button, proceeds with cancellation)

**On confirmation:**
- Update the booking's `payment_status` to `refunded` (or create a new status `cancelled` if you prefer)
- If the game was previously `full`, update its status back to `open`
- Free up the player's slot so other players can book it
- Show a confirmation message: "Your booking has been cancelled. [Refund details based on tier]."
- Redirect back to `/my-games`

**Important:** The refund amount shown should be the game price + service fee (the total the player paid). The actual refund percentage applies to that total.

---

### 3. Post-Payment Confirmation Screen

After a successful payment, before redirecting to the game page, show a confirmation screen at `/payment/callback` (or enhance the existing one):

**Confirmation screen content:**
- Large green checkmark icon at the top
- "Booking Confirmed!" in large bold text
- Game title
- Date and kickoff time
- Pitch name
- Team assignment (Team 1 or Team 2)
- Amount paid (SAR XX)
- A "View Game" button linking to `/game/[id]`
- A "My Games" button linking to `/my-games`

This screen satisfies the Amazon Payment Services requirement that the customer receives an on-screen payment confirmation with booking details.

---

### 4. Navigation Update

Add a "My Games" link to the navbar for logged-in players. Show it next to the language toggle or in a user menu. Do not show it for guests (not logged in) or for hosts/organisers.

---

### 5. Game Detail Page Update (`/game/[id]`)

When a logged-in player has a paid booking for this game, show a "Cancel Booking" link or button somewhere on the page (e.g. below the "You're in · Team X" badge in the sticky bottom bar, or as a small text link). This opens the same cancellation modal described above.
